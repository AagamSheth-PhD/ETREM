The Naked Truth

Raw. Real. Unfiltered.

ETREM was born from a simple, radical idea: what if perfume was just about the perfume? In an industry cluttered with celebrity endorsements, extravagant packaging, and baffling markups, we decided to strip it all away. We removed the layers of pretense to focus on the one thing that truly matters — the scent itself.

We believe luxury is not about the price tag, but the experience. It's the confidence a great fragrance gives you, the memories it evokes, and the way it becomes a part of your skin. That's why we're called "The Naked Perfume." No gimmicks. No over-packaging. No inflated prices. Just pure, honest, long-lasting fragrance crafted for those who want the scent, not the brand tax.

This is fragrance, unfiltered.

— Neema Dhaval Dabhi, Founder of absolutist.

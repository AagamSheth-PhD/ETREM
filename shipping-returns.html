Shipping Policy

We offer a range of shipping options. All available methods and their costs are shown at checkout.

Standard delivery within India takes 5 to 7 business days. Delivery times may vary during peak seasons or to remote locations.

Orders are processed and dispatched within 2–3 business days of being placed. Remote areas may take longer.

A tracking number will be sent to your registered email address as soon as your order has been shipped.

Fees are calculated based on the shipping method, package weight, and destination. The final amount is displayed at checkout.

We currently offer shipping to select countries. Please check availability during the checkout process.

You will receive email notifications at each stage: order confirmation, shipment, and delivery.

Returns & Refunds Policy

Returns are accepted within 7 days of purchase. The item must be in its original condition, unworn, and with all tags attached.

Non-returnable items: Personalized items, intimate apparel, and items marked as "Final Sale".

Refunds are processed within 5 business days to the original payment method after the returned item passes inspection.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a defective product.

For size or variant exchanges, please contact our customer service team to arrange the process.

If you have an account, log in, go to your Order History, select the order, and follow the instructions. For guest returns, please email us at absolutist.inc@gmail.com.

The customer bears the cost of return shipping unless the item is defective or we made an error with the order.

Please contact us within 1 day of receiving a damaged or defective item. We will arrange a replacement or a full refund.

Please allow up to 5 business days for the refund amount to appear in your account after processing.

For any questions, email us at absolutist.inc@gmail.com.
